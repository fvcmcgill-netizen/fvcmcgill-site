Our Mission

The purpose of FVC McGill is to provide an opportunity for students to be involved with veterinary life through events and seminars from various schools. Our goal is to unite the many keen students who wish to pursue veterinary medicine and provide them with a safe space to make lasting friendships. We want to showcase the options available for students to pursue after McGill and hopefully make the grad school application process less daunting.

Want to become part of the FVC community?

We’re open to all McGill students with an interest in veterinary sciences.
