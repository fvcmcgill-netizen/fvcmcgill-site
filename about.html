About FVC McGill

The Future Veterinarian Club (FVC) at McGill is a student-run organization dedicated to building a vibrant, supportive community for students passionate about veterinary science and animal health. Whether you're preparing for vet school or just curious about the field, our club offers resources, mentorship, and events to help you grow and explore your interest.

We host guest speakers, skill-building workshops, student panels, field trips, and more. We also aim to foster connections between members and with professionals in the field.

Our Values

Community

We believe in supporting one another through shared learning and encouragement.

Curiosity

We explore all aspects of veterinary medicine — academic and practical.

Commitment

We strive to provide resources and opportunities that reflect our shared passion.
